Regulatory context

Why this matters right now

China's 15th Five-Year Plan (2026–2030) introduces a dual-control system for carbon emissions, and chemicals/petrochemicals are slated for Emissions Trading Scheme coverage by 2027. National standards for plastics carbon footprint accounting and recycled-content labeling took effect February 2026. Suppliers with batch-level carbon data and recycled content already documented will have a head start once buyer-side reporting obligations tighten.
